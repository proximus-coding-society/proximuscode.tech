What We Do

We collaborate to learn, un-learn and re-learn.

Lectures

Lecture on various technologies and doubt debugging sessions are given to students along with needfull resources to learn.

Monthly Competitions

Competitions are conducted every month in preferred domains by students to hone their skills.

Bootcamps

Bootcamps are conducted,foussing on new and trending languages and technologies.

Hackathons

Design sprint-like event in which Students in a team involve in software development, including graphic designing, interface designing, project managing and unleash their problem solving skills.

Internships

Various Internships on Development and Research can be earned based on ones skills and competance.

App Development

We conducts sessions/webinars to help students to make apps for android/ios platform.

Events

We organize different unparalleled events like PMoC, Projecathon, Games-Of-Code.

PMoC 💻

Proximus Month of Code, is a program for the students held by Proximus to encourage students to take part in the Open Source Development.

  • An initiative to make students comfortable for GSoC.
  • Mentors are assigned to help students.
  • Certificates are provided after the succesful submission of projects.

Projecathon 👩‍💻

Projecathon is a program to give a taste of hackathon to students which lasts for 4-5 days, in which students are assigned some projects and a proper team.

  • Helps in your Internship.
  • Winning team is provided with a paid project.
  • It is fun!

Game of Codes 🤖

Monthly programming contest held to master and improve the data structures and algorithms.

  • Held on an online judge.
  • Prepare yourself for prestigious competitions like ACM.
  • Rank Holders get goodies and prizes too!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is Proximus?

Proximus is a non profit college based society started by the Nitish at HMRITM. We are team of bunch passionated people to contribute to the society , We at Proximus believe that everyone can learn to code and should know how to code, or inshort we can promise you that it can be a life changing experience here at Proximus.

Why should i join Proximus?

If you ever dreamt to be at an ivy league college, but ended up at a tier-2 college, and don't feel motivated enough to do something, then without giving it a second thought, you should apply to join Proximus. Which can help you to be at par of those studying at Tier-1 Colleges, doing wonders in international olympiads and much more.

Is Proximus Free?

Yes, Proximus is free and always will be. Our, primary motto is to build a community to help students getting placed at big giants, to grab up good job opportunities, to help Students to discover their inner potential to do wonders.

If I need support who do I contact?

Feel free to contact your designated proximus class coordinator or Drop us an email to info@proximuscode.tech and some of our teammate will get back to you shortly.

About Us

Proximus Coding Society, a non-profit college based society founded in 2016 by Nitish Sahani.
